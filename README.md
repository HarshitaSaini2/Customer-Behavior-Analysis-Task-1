# 🛍️ E-Commerce Customer Analytics

This project analyzes **250,000 e-commerce transactions** from **49,673 customers** to uncover customer behavior, purchasing patterns, and retention opportunities. Using **Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Scikit-learn, and Jupyter Notebook**, the project performs data cleaning, feature engineering, customer segmentation, and business intelligence analysis.

## 🚀 Project Objectives

* Clean and preprocess raw e-commerce transaction data.
* Analyze customer purchasing behavior and sales trends.
* Perform **RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)** customer segmentation.
* Apply **K-Means Clustering** to identify customer groups.
* Analyze customer retention and churn behavior.
* Provide actionable business recommendations to improve customer engagement and revenue.

## 📊 Key Features

## 📈 Insights

* High-value loyal customers contribute the largest share of total revenue.
* Dormant and hibernating customers represent the biggest retention opportunity.
* Customer retention drops significantly after the first two months.
* Clothing and Books generate the highest order volume, while Electronics and Home produce higher revenue per order.
* Behavioral metrics such as Recency are more reliable indicators of churn than the provided churn label.

## 📌 Business Recommendations

* Launch targeted win-back campaigns for inactive customers.
* Improve the first 90-day customer onboarding journey.
* Introduce loyalty programs for high-value customers.
* Use personalized product recommendations for cross-selling.
* Reduce product returns by improving product information and customer experience.

## 📂 Project Output

The project includes detailed visualizations, customer segmentation analysis, retention reports, and business recommendations that can help e-commerce companies improve customer satisfaction, retention, and overall revenue growth.
